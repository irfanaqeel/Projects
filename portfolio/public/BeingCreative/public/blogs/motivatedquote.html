Quotes That Will Make You Motivated

It's really hard to be motivated all the time because sometimes we have situations that break us by inside and we think that giving up is the better option yet. The enthusiasm of our body which is very high in the time of sunrise getting low at the time of sunset, but we have to do some extra stuff to achieve our goals.
Here’s a list of inspirational quotes that always get me off my but. I found that it works best to print your favorite quotes and place them somewhere you see them multiple times a day. So there are some quotes that will make motivated and feel compelled to accomplish.


  1. Our greatest weakness lies in giving up. The most certain way to succeed is always to try just one more time.-Thomas. A Edison
    Before giving up just ask a question to yourself that why you start? How did you think that you are worthy for it once you get the answers you will find your way.

  2. You must expect great things of yourself before you can do them.-Michael Jordan
    If you do not believe in yourself you won't be achieve anything in your life. No matter how many people tell you that you're great and you will do many great things until and unless you make it worth by yourself

  3. Obstacles are necessary for success because in selling, as in all careers of importance, victory comes only after many struggles and countless defeats.-Og Mandino
    The winner is not the one who wins but the one who defeats because he knows the actual worth of victory. So the obstacles are really necessary for us because a baby always falls before start running. So, unless you face obstruction until you won't get the victory.

  4. It is during our darkest moments that we must focus to see the light.-Aristotle Onassis
    Don't worry when the situation is getting difficult and there's no way to get it out, keep calm and looking for the light. Focus on what you're doing and you will succeed because only in the darkness you can see the stars.

  5. The only thing we have to fear is fear itself.-Franklin D. Roosevelt
    Fear is not real it is an image that we create in our mind. We don't fear going into the darkness the fear is what is in the darkness. But it's good to be afraid before start something because it means that you're ready.

  6. The greater danger for most of us isn’t that our aim is too high and miss it, but that it is too low and we reach it.-Michelangelo
    I agree that is important to set goals in life but it is more important to set goals that are more unattainable to achieve. You live only once you don't waste it to set goals which are easy to achieve think big. There's another saying that "Shoot for the moon and you might hit the stars".I will not follow where the path may lead, but I will go where there is no path.

  7. A goal properly set is halfway reached.-Zig Ziglar

    Setting a goal in life is the most important thing and when you set it you halfway reached your goal. You know what your aim of life and what you want to be. Goal-setting is a useful and tried-and-tested way of working toward achieving something specific. They are often referred to as stepping stones towards your purpose.

  8. Plans are nothing; planning is everything.-Dwight D. Eisenhower
    It is more important to know the difference between Plan VS Planning. The plan is just a documentation of your planning while planning is an active way of discussing the goals, objectives, strategies, and tasks that we need to accomplish. Plans need to update on a regular base.

  9. Learning never exhausts the mind.-Leonardo Da Vinci

    Reading is learning and learning is the only thing the mind never exhausts, never fears, and never regret. What you're learning today is definitely helps you tomorrow because of knowledge man becomes wise and successful. So, read as much as you can.

  10. Your time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life.Steve Jobs
    Your life is too short so please don't waste it to think that what other people say about you and your life because if you think what other people think so what other people think and that is not really your business so live your life in your own way and believe yourself, and one day you'll say I did it.

    So there are some famous quotes which will make you motivated. Last but not least the most important thing is Quote won't work unless you do.
