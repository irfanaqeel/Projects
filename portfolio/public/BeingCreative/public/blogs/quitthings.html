1. Trying To Please Everyone.

I don't know the key to success, but the key to failure is trying to please everybody. If you're pleasing everyone then it might be start time to say "No".

2. Fearing Change.

Fearing change is the enemy of success. If you want to be successful in your life then do not fear changing, change fear.

People fear change because they lose control over their roles. The larger the change, the more they are going to feel like the change is being done to them and no one likes feeling powerless.

Yes, I have a past but I don't live to their anymore and I know that everyone has a past and it's really bad but it doesn't that what happened to you in your past the things will be the same as in future.

To get over the past you have to accept that because you can't master your future if you are still a slave of your past so, don't worry and focus on your present and try to make it better than your past and you'll see your best comes.


4. Putting Yourself Down.

Life is unpredictable, anything can happen but no matter what life throws at you, you just not to put your self down. Putting down yourself means that you believe you never get that what you deserve and you suddenly you may lose hope.

Even the most positive and confident people put down themselves but they were full of self-esteem. Whenever you feel that you just say out loud one thing in your head is"Stop I can do this and I will". And just believe yourself you're more than what you think.
